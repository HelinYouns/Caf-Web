About

VERO COFFEE

Vero Coffee has been proudly serving since 2020, founded and owned by Ali. The name "Vero" is derived from Italian, meaning "true" — a reflection of our dedication to offering authentic, high-quality coffee experiences.

Vero Coffee began as a small shop in Rahimawa, Kirkuk, and thanks to the love and support of our community, we’ve grown into two thriving branches.

Every cup we serve is crafted with care, combining rich flavors, cozy vibes, and a passion for making your day better — one coffee at a time.
